Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Safe Haven Equity is a lead generation website. We collect your personal details solely to connect you with qualified equity release advisers. We do not provide financial advice through this website.

1. Who We Are

Safe Haven Equity ("we", "us", "our") operates this website as a lead generation service. Our role is to collect enquiries from people interested in equity release and pass those enquiries to qualified, regulated equity release advisers who may contact you to discuss your options.

For questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: 020 8087 4645 or via the contact form on our website.

2. What Information We Collect

When you submit an enquiry through our website, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your age
  • An estimate of your property value
  • Your preferred time to be contacted

We also collect standard technical data when you visit our website, such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This is used for website analytics and security only.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Review your enquiry and assess whether it is suitable to pass to a qualified adviser
  • Pass your details to a qualified, regulated equity release adviser who may contact you
  • Respond to any questions or requests you send us directly
  • Improve our website and services

We will only use your information for the purpose you submitted it. We will not use your data for unrelated marketing without your explicit consent.

4. Who We Share Your Information With

If your enquiry is suitable, we will pass your details to a qualified equity release adviser. That adviser is a separate, independent business and will be regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA). They will identify themselves and explain their regulatory status when they contact you.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not share your data with any business unrelated to your equity release enquiry.

We may also share your data with trusted service providers who help us operate this website (for example, our form processing provider), under strict data protection agreements.

5. How Long We Keep Your Data

We keep your enquiry data for up to 24 months from the date of submission. After that period, it is securely deleted unless we are required to retain it for legal or compliance reasons.

6. Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data
  • Object to how we use your data
  • Request that we restrict processing of your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at 020 8087 4645. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to help it function and to understand how visitors use it. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect how some parts of the website work.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal data from loss, misuse, and unauthorised access. Our form submissions are processed securely and your data is never stored in plain text. However, no internet transmission is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will always show when it was last updated. Continued use of our website after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Phone: 020 8087 4645
  • Website: safehavenequity.co.uk

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your data, you have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk or by calling 0303 123 1113.
