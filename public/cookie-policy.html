Cookie Policy

How Welsh Dragon Rugby uses cookies and similar technologies.

Last updated: 24 May 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which ones Welsh Dragon Rugby uses, and what your choices are. By continuing to use this site, you agree to our use of cookies as described below.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, phone or tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit — such as your preferences — and help the site work properly.

Cookies cannot harm your device and do not contain any personally identifying information on their own.

What Cookies We Use

Welsh Dragon Rugby uses the following types of cookies:

Type Purpose Examples
Essential Cookies Required for the website to function correctly. These cannot be disabled. Session management, security
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. Data is anonymous and aggregated. Google Analytics (_ga, _gid)
Advertising Cookies Used to show relevant adverts. If Google AdSense is active on this site, Google may set advertising cookies. Google AdSense (IDE, DSID)
Preference Cookies Remember your choices and settings to improve your experience. Display preferences

Google AdSense and Advertising

Welsh Dragon Rugby may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to Welsh Dragon Rugby and/or other sites on the internet.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with Welsh Dragon Rugby. This helps us improve the site over time. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous data about page visits, time on site and general user behaviour.

No personally identifiable information is collected through Google Analytics. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Third Party Cookies

Some content or features on Welsh Dragon Rugby may be provided by third parties. These third parties may also set cookies on your device. We do not control the use of these cookies and recommend checking the privacy policies of those third parties directly.

Current third parties whose services may set cookies include:

  • Google (Analytics and AdSense)
  • API-Sports (live sports data provider)

How to Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Please be aware that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of Welsh Dragon Rugby and other websites you visit.

For guidance on managing cookies in your browser, visit aboutcookies.org.
