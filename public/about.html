About Welsh Dragon Rugby

Your home for Welsh rugby — live scores, results, standings, and news covering the teams that matter most to Welsh fans.

What We Do

Welsh Dragon Rugby is an independent website dedicated to following Welsh rugby at every level. From the Welsh national team to the regional sides competing in the URC and European competitions, we bring together live data, results, and news in one place.

We're not affiliated with the WRU or any of the regional clubs — we're simply passionate Welsh rugby fans who wanted a clean, fast, modern site that puts the rugby front and centre.
